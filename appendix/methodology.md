# Methodology

## Analytical Approach

The case uses a hypothesis-driven consulting workflow:

1. Define the decision question.
2. Identify the candidate universe from public sources.
3. Build a MECE issue tree.
4. Establish the financial baseline.
5. Separate temporary Product Commerce pressure from structural growth-investment drag.
6. Map initiative-level evidence.
7. Score options under one standard.
8. Translate the scorecard into gates and recommendations.

The candidate universe and time-horizon rationale are documented in [candidate_universe.md](candidate_universe.md).

## Why A Scorecard Instead Of Full NPV

A full NPV/IRR model would require internal data that is not public:

- Taiwan standalone revenue, gross margin, EBITDA, cohort retention, fulfillment density, and capex.
- Eats contribution margin, subsidy intensity, delivery cost, order density, and incremental LTV.
- WOW member count, churn, benefit cost, and incremental spend.
- Farfetch standalone revenue, gross margin, adjusted EBITDA, restructuring savings, and integration milestones.

Because those data are unavailable, the case uses a weighted decision scorecard. The scorecard is meant to support prioritization, not valuation.

## Scoring Criteria

| Criterion | Question |
| --- | --- |
| Economics | Does the option protect or create margin capacity? |
| Strategic Fit | Does the option reinforce Coupang's fulfillment, frequency, data, and membership flywheel? |
| Execution Feasibility | Can Coupang execute this option without excessive complexity or distraction? |
| Evidence Strength | How much of the thesis is supported by public official evidence? |
| Reversibility | Can management scale, hold, slow, or stop the option as evidence changes? |

## Decision Bands

| Score | Posture | Meaning |
| ---: | --- | --- |
| 75-100 | Scale / Protect | Allocate capital and management attention with normal governance. |
| 60-74 | Selective Scale | Fund in stages with explicit gates. |
| 45-59 | Hold / Prove | Keep option alive but require proof before incremental scale. |
| <45 | Constrain / Turnaround | Limit capital; require turnaround evidence or exit review. |

## Quality Controls

- Every major number is tied to official public sources.
- Missing data are labeled as gaps.
- Management commentary is separated from disclosed financial facts.
- Directional inferences are not presented as verified facts.
- Sensitivity is discussed at the ranking level.
